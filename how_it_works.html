How It Works


In our agency each perspective member is asked to complete a personal profile form (questionnaire) of 35 questions that helps us build your profile and to get to know you and understand your needs.

When your application has been approved, you will be contacted for some additional information and we will ask you send us an short introductory video about yourself, just to become more familiar with you – what you look like, what you sound like and what your personality is like. (don’t worry a package about you will be kept confidentially and not will be share with a potential match before you not give a permission)

After everything is set, we will start looking for your perfect match and setting up your dates. When we have a potential match for you, Laura will contact you to discuss if you are interested in meeting.

Please know in all cases we work together, and you are always in complete control of who you want to meet.

What is your next step? When both parties have agreed to meet up – it's time get ready for your first date.

For your convenience we have two options here for you, you can choose one where you feel more comfortable.:

Option 1
  • Exchange phone numbers and we will ask the gentlemen to make the initial call to set up your first date.
(just you are empowered to make you own arrangements of suitable time and date)

Option 2
  • Or Leave it with us and we will arrange your meeting at the suitable time, date and a venue that suits both of you. So, you go and enjoy yourself with a date carefully selected for you by our dating specialist Laura.
After your date we would like to hear your feedback from both sides. Share your experience with us.

Are you still in doubt? Don’t wait any longer do your first step, and we will help you to find a life partner for you. Start completing your personal profile form now or give us a call to find out more about our matching and dating service.
